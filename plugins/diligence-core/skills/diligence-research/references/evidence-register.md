# Evidence register

| ID | Claim | Type | Source / location | Source date | Accessed | Quality | Supports / contradicts | Confidence | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Allowed `Type` values: fact, calculation, inference, assumption, management claim, open question.

For calculations, add:

| Metric | Formula | Inputs and sources | Result | Sensitivity / caveat |
|---|---|---|---|---|

For unresolved conflicts, add:

| Topic | Evidence A | Evidence B | Likely cause | Resolution step | Decision impact |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
