# The Diligence Stack: access and billing

## Product description

The Diligence Stack is a research publication from Creative Strategies, distributed through Substack. It covers the core fundamentals of companies, technologies, and industries as a due-diligence resource for the full stack.

AI is the primary organizing lens because it connects changes in semiconductors, infrastructure, cloud platforms, software, customer workflows, budgets, and business models. The coverage can extend beyond AI whenever full-stack technical, market, and economic analysis is needed.

The research is designed to clarify systems, mechanisms, assumptions, and competitive position. It does not provide personalized investment advice, price targets, ratings, or subscriber-specific conclusions.

## Access levels

### Free reader

- Receives the opening 600-800 words of each report through the publication.
- Does not receive subscriber portal or MCP access merely by installing this plugin.

### Paid subscriber

- Receives full subscriber research and archive access.
- Can sign into the private subscriber portal using the subscriber identity verification flow.
- Can authorize the read-only Diligence Stack MCP and access the shared `diligence-stack-reports` corpus.

### Private equity-research entitlement

- Is limited to accounts that have been explicitly granted access.
- Is exposed as the `equity-research` knowledge-base key only when present in the user's OAuth grant.
- Is not guaranteed by plugin installation or ordinary paid-subscriber status.
- Must not be copied into public artifacts or shared with people who lack access.

## Billing guidance

- Installing the plugin is not a purchase and does not activate research access.
- Diligence Stack subscription billing is handled through The Diligence Stack/Substack, not Codex, ChatGPT, Claude, or the MCP server.
- As of June 19, 2026, direct web plan metadata lists USD 300 monthly or USD 3,000 annually.
- Prices, local currency, taxes, promotions, group terms, and app-store prices may differ or change. Direct users to the [live subscription page](https://www.thediligencestack.com/subscribe) for the current offer.
- Codex/ChatGPT or Claude subscriptions and model usage are separate charges governed by those providers.
- OAuth only authorizes an eligible account; it does not itself create a charge or upgrade a subscription.

## Support language

If authorization fails:

1. Confirm the user has a paid Diligence Stack subscription under the email used for the portal.
2. Have the user open [The Diligence Stack](https://www.thediligencestack.com/) and choose the subscriber sign-in flow to complete email or passkey verification.
3. Retry the MCP OAuth connection from Codex or Claude.
4. If the shared corpus works but `equity-research` is absent, explain that it is a separate private entitlement rather than an OAuth failure.

Do not promise refunds, upgrades, private access, or specific tax treatment. Route account-specific billing and entitlement questions through the support path shown in the subscriber portal or publication.
