# Model audit checklist

## Structure

- [ ] Scope, period, frequency, currency, and units are explicit.
- [ ] Every material output has a visible driver chain.
- [ ] Assumptions have one canonical location.
- [ ] Historical facts and forecasts are visually or structurally distinct.

## Evidence

- [ ] Every material sourced input has an evidence ID, date, and locator.
- [ ] Private research is labeled and not placed in a shareable output.
- [ ] Derived estimates and analyst assumptions are not presented as facts.
- [ ] Stale or missing evidence is disclosed.

## Mechanics

- [ ] Totals reconcile and signs are consistent.
- [ ] Percentages, currencies, units, and time periods are normalized.
- [ ] Base/upside/downside ordering behaves logically.
- [ ] Zero, negative, delay, and constraint cases do not break formulas.
- [ ] Sensitivities change only intended drivers.

## Interpretation

- [ ] The highest-impact assumptions are identified.
- [ ] Correlated scenario assumptions are not double counted.
- [ ] Falsification signals and update triggers are documented.
- [ ] Precision does not exceed evidence quality.
